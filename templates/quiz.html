Depression Screening Quiz

1. How often do you feel sad or empty?





2. How interested are you in activities you used to enjoy?





3. How is your appetite?





4. How would you describe your sleep patterns?





5. How often do you feel hopeless about the future?





6. How would you rate your energy levels?





7. How often do you feel guilty or worthless?





8. How would you describe your concentration?





9. How often do you think about death or suicide?





10. How would you rate your overall mood?
